Background

The quick story of how I fell in love with building software and supporting teams.

Systems thinker

I gravitate toward backend problems because they reward deliberate design. From database schemas to automation pipelines, I prefer solutions that are simple to reason about and easy to hand off.

Collaborative learner

Whether pair programming or documenting a tricky concept, I like sharing the knowledge that helps teams move faster. That is why the blog exists and why I keep detailed runbooks.

Values

Principles that guide how I work.

  • Ship clarity. Every project deserves clear naming, reliable deployment, and documentation you can trust on a stressful day.
  • Be considerate. People make software. The best tooling respects their time and their focus.
  • Stay curious. I set aside time each week to explore something new—lately Typst, Bun, and more effective testing strategies.
